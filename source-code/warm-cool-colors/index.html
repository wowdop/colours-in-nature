Warm colors are: reds, oranges and yellows.

  • Warm colors are generally associated with energy, brightness, action, coziness and energy.
  • Brown is generally associated with masculinity and yellow with competence and happiness.
  • These colors are said to bring to mind warmth, like the sun.

Cool colors are: blues, greens and purples.

  • Cool colors are often identified with calm, peace, serenity and isolation.
  • Blue is generally associated with dependability.
  • These colors are said to bring to mind coolness, like water.
